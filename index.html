National Parks

I love to hike, and I love U.S. National Parks as well. My favorite National Park is the Great Smoky Mountains National Park in Gatlinburg, Tennessee. I have only been there twice, but I really enjoyed my time there both times. I think it was better to go during early summer or late spring. This is when the smokiness is most apparent, and the sights are beautiful. The hikes were pretty strenuous, but the view makes it absolutely worth.
